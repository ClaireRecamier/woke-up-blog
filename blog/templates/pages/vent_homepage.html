What is Venting?

- Means to blow of steam, usually is done when you're angry and/or stressed.
- You could also do it when you just need to get something off your chest.

Why should you vent on this site?

- Sometimes people don't want to be judged about what they might say or don't feel comfortable saying it, so they might keep a lot to themselves.
- By saying what you have to say on this website, you are anonymous and don't have to worry about that.
- Also your post will go away in 48 hours

- Express Yourself and Stay Positive.
